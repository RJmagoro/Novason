Frequently asked questions

  1. Q:What is the minimum waiting period?
    A: The minimum waiting period is 6 calendar months.
  2. Q: What if I commit Suicide?
    A: A 24 months waiting period applies
  3. Q: What is the age limit?
    A: We cover members between the age of 18 and 90 years of age and a 12 months waiting period applies to members who are between the ages of 86 to 90 years
  4. Q: Who can I cover?
    A: You can cover yourself & Spouse including children under 21 years of age.
  5. Q: What is the waiting period on accidental death?
    A: We cover accidental death immediately
  6. Q: What is the waiting period for a payout?
    A: All Valid claims payout within 6 hours
  7. Q: Can I cover my parent's?
    A: You can cover your parents including (siblings uncle & aunt) as extended family members, at an additional cost.
  8. Q: What if I adopt children, can they also be covered?
    A: All Legally adopted children will be covered ( up to a maximum of 7 children)
  9. Q: What if I miss payment?
    A:The plan will not pay-out if premiums are in arrears or short paid
  10. Q: Who can take this cover.
    A: Any South African citizen or foreign national
